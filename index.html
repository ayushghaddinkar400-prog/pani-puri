Simple Pani Puri Recipe

Ingredients (for 20 puris):

  • 20 puris (store-bought or homemade)
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and diced
  • 1 cup chickpeas, boiled
  • 1 cup tamarind chutney
  • 1 cup mint chutney
  • Spiced water: 4 cups water, 1 tbsp chaat masala, 1 tsp cumin, salt, lemon juice

Instructions:

  1. Prepare spiced water by mixing ingredients and chilling.
  2. Crack a small hole in each puri.
  3. Fill with potatoes, chickpeas, and chutneys.
  4. Pour in spiced water and eat immediately!
